FAQs

Which cities do you deliver in?

Dooze is available for delivery in Kolkata as of now. But we are currently expanding to other cities as well.

Can I order from any location?

Youll only be able to place an order from your location if weve licensed liquor retailers listed on our platform from that locality or nearby areas.

How to contact Dooze for any more queries?

You can reach out to us on our Toll-Free No. 1800-121-4467 or mail us on [email protected] for more queries.

Why do you ask for my Date of Birth?

We require your date of birth at the time of registration to verify your Legal drinking age. As per the excise regulations, we are only allowed to deliver alcohol to users who are of Legal drinking age in the state of residence.

Do I need an ID Proof?

Yes, you will need an ID proof to complete one time KYC before placing an order, as it is mandatory to be of Legal Drinking Age.
